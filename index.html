Industrial Project with Optiver

During the course of our project, we spearheaded the creation and refinement of a platform dedicated to predicting stock market volatility. Notably, the kNN model stood out, delivering exceptional results and underscoring our expertise in crafting precise forecasting tools.

Sydney Livability Analysis

Club Website

The analysis of Sydney's livability is derived from the formula \( g(z) = \frac{1}{{1 + e^{-z}}} \), where \( z \) represents the z-score and \( e \) is the exponential constant. To compute the livability score for each neighborhood, we aggregate the z-scores from various datasets: \( z_{\text{school}} + z_{\text{accommodation}} + z_{\text{retail}} - z_{\text{crime}} + z_{\text{health}} \). The z-score for each dataset is calculated as \( \frac{x - \text{mean}}{\text{standard deviation}} \). Our analysis encompasses data on accommodation prices, school enrollment, crime rates, health metrics, and retail statistics. Once visualized on a map, the most yellow regions indicate the most desirable places to live in Sydney!
